Benign Lootbox Statements

A satire of over-exaggerated social media influencer advertising of in-game purchases.

    Skills

  • 3D Modelling
  • Coding

    Software

  • Blender
  • Visual Studio Code

An instagram page exploring video game loot boxes, but instead of receiving flashy prizes, you get user generated responses of why they may have gone to open one more.
I wanted to flip the expectation of a loot box, the flashy lights, the saturated colors and hope of hitting a big prize, and make it benign.
While it was never implemented, I wanted to have it so people could have their own experiences shared through a twitter page and added to the instagram page.
